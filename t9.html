9. Roxas Night Market


In other places, nightlife is about bars, cocktails, and ladies in bikinis.
But not here in Davao. The Roxas Night Market will help you define what
“night life” is in Davao City. Instead of ladies in two-piece bikinis, you
will see women grilling seafood and barbecues (that includes chicken intestine,
chicken head, chicken feet, pork barbecue, longganiza, etc.) You can also try
Mang Danny’s Ice Cream, according to the opinion of those who have tried, the
dirty ice cream tastes like a gelato. You can also enjoy buying merchandises
in a bargain.

The Roxas Night Market is one of the best places in Davao where you can enjoy
being one with the Dabawenyos (the people lives in Davao). The items and street
food are cheap!

Address: Roxas Ave, Poblacion District, Davao City,
Davao del Sur, Philippines
