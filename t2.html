2. Davao Crocodile Park


Not far away from the city proper, you can visit Davao Crocodile Park,
the place where you will find delight in nature and wild. This park does
not only have crocs but tigers and other variety of animals as well.

Technically, it is a zoo, but not the typical kind of zoo. The Davao
Crocodile Park also offers several shows such as tightrope walking, where
a staff will walk on a tightrope over wild crocs.

After a plunge in the noisy, urban city of Davao, a visit to the Crocodile
Park will take the stress away from you. The breathtaking shows along with
the sight of adorable animals will entertain you and will make you love
nature once again.

Address: Gadi Road, Talomo, Davao City, Davao del Sur, Philippines
