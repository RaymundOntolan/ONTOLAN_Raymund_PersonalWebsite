4. Talicud Island


An island behind Samal is the Talicud Island. The islet is yet unknown to
several travelers. But I feel obliged to tell you, Talicud Island is a place
never shall you miss! One of the favorite resorts in Talicud Island is the
Isla Reta, a simple resort that offers an economical stay to keep you cozy
through the night. There is nothing really much to do on the Island, but you
can dive the beach and enjoy the magnificent sea creatures that you will see.
You will definitely enjoy snorkeling! At night, you can rent a single sleeping
mattress and place it in the seashore. Forget the city lights and enjoy
star-gazing the vast night sky.
