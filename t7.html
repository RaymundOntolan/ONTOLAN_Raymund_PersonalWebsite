7. Rodrigo Duterte’s House


Does Pres. Rodrigo Duterte ring a bell? Sure he does! He is Philippine’s president,
an empowering leader, admired by lots of Filipinos because of his robust and
tough-talking personality and his firm principles. Before he became the President,
he served as a Mayor of Davao City for seven terms.

The President’s house is rather simple and not luxurious. As you visit his home,
you will see the simplicity he has had. You can also take a tour inside and see his
bed with Kulambo (mosquito net).
