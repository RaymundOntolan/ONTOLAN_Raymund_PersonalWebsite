3. Pearl Farm Beach Resort


A quiet cove in the Island Garden of Samal, unlike other resorts Pearl Farm
will give you an exclusive getaway experience without the noise of beachgoers.

The resort offers warm accommodation. The rooms are in the contour of the
island to give guests a delightful and awe-inspiring view of the horizon where
the blue beach and cobalt sky meet. The resort was formerly known for cultivating
pearls.

As for today, Pearl Farm Beach Resort offers a variety of fun water sports and
activities such as water polo, jet skiing, and scuba diving. A game room is also
available for sports enthusiasts who would love to play basketball, badminton,
or tennis.

Pearl Farm Beach Resort offers relaxation and diversion that the city cannot provide.
It is a place not only for leisure but also for getting attached to the countryside.
