1. Philippine Eagle Center


The Filipino eagle is a very rare and endangered species living in the Davao province in Philippines. One hour from the city proper, you will reach the Philippine Eagle
Center (PEC), the home of the Grand monkey-eating eagle that can only be
found in the Philippines.

Not only you will have a close look at the great eagle, but also to other
rare species such as sea eagle, hawks, along with other mammals and reptiles.
The PEC is a home of 36 Philippine Eagles.

Visiting PEC, you would have an incredible experience having to encounter
these rare species. It is also a wonderful experience to visit the safe haven
of these extinct animals.
