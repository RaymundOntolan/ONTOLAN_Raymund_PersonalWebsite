point of 9,700 ft. above sea level. The beautiful boulders of Mt. Apo along
with the canopies of green pasture and grass and the colorful faunas, you
will surely enjoy trekking the mountain. You will be enchanted with the
awe-inspiring, breathtaking epic sunrise in the morning as well as the
spectacular sunset that will surely give you an unexplainable pleasure
and adrenaline rush.
