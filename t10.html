10. Water Rafting in Davao River


If you want another wild adventure, you can visit the Davao River. From the
city proper, you will have to travel for an hour going to the west to reach
the genesis of the river. You will experience an hour of water rapids, splash,
and capsizes. Water rafting is best enjoyed with friends or family. This activity
is ideal for those who are looking for extreme venture!
