8. Museo Dabawenyo


There are two museums in Davao City, the Museo Dabawenyo and the Davao
Museum which is slightly bigger than the first one. If you want to know
more about the culture and ethnography of Davao City, a visit to Museo Dabawenyo
shall be your first step. The museum shows the art, music, instruments, and
weapons carved and made by the indigenous people of Davao City.
Address: Andres Bonifacio Rotunda, Poblacion District, Davao City,
Davao del Sur, Philippines
